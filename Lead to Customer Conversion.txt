Lead to Customer Conversion
Lead Pipeline
1. New Lead 
No contact made. 
2. Working Lead 
Contact established and engagement started. 
3. Qualified Lead 
Lead verified and meets qualification criteria. 
4. Unqualified Lead 
Not a good fit or does not meet criteria. 
Lead Conversion
Qualified Lead → Account + Contact + Opportunity

Opportunity (Sales) Pipeline
1. Prospecting 
Initial outreach and contact. 
2. Qualification 
Verify prospect information and fit. 
3. Need Analysis 
Understand requirements and pain points. 
4. Value Proposition 
Present solution and business value. 
5. Perception Analysis 
Assess how the prospect views your offering vs competitors. 
6. Decision Making 
Handle approvals, negotiations, and final decision. 
7. Closed Won / Closed Lost 
Customer acquired or opportunity lost. 
Flow
New Lead → Working Lead → Qualified Lead → Convert → Account + Contact + Opportunity → Prospecting → Qualification → Need Analysis → Value Proposition → Perception Analysis → Decision Making → Closed Won/Lost
